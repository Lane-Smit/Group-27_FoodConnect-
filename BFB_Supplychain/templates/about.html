About FoodConnect

FoodConnect is a South African digital platform dedicated to transforming the way surplus food is managed, distributed, and valued. Our mission is to connect food suppliers and communities in need by creating a seamless, digital connection between farmers, shops, bakeries, restaurants, and recipient organisations such as underprivileged schools, shelters, and NGOs.

Every year, approximately 10 million tonnes of edible food is wasted in South Africa while many face hunger. FoodConnect was developed to change that. By providing real-time visibility of surplus food and automating redistribution, we make it easier for businesses to identify those in need and redirect food before it expires. This not only reduces waste but also ensures that nutritious food is provided to those in need.

Through innovation and collaboration, FoodConnect empowers suppliers to take action toward a more sustainable and socially responsible food supply chain. The platform features a user-friendly dashboard, live inventory tracking, and data insights that promote efficiency, accountability, and sustainable resource management.

At FoodConnect, we believe that food should never be wasted when it can feed families in need. We aim to protect the environment while also supporting our underserved communities. By connecting suppliers to those in need, we are helping South Africa build a more sustainable food supply system, one connection at a time. Join us in redefining how South Africans turn food surplus into food security.
